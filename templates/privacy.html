Privacy Policy

ConComply is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. What We Collect

  • Email addresses for account access
  • Submitted documents and related metadata (e.g., file name, type, submission date)
  • Basic usage logs to monitor service health

2. Use of Information

We use the information solely to operate and improve the service. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

All documents are stored securely on AWS S3. Access to files is restricted and temporarily authorized via secure links.

4. Personal Information

Some documents may contain names or certification data. We treat such data with the same care as sensitive content.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request the deletion of your data at any time by emailing beta.concomply@gmail.com.

6. Disclaimer

This is a beta version. We cannot guarantee continuous access, permanent storage, or full protection against unforeseen security risks.

7. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the service evolves. Continued use of ConComply implies acceptance of the latest version.
